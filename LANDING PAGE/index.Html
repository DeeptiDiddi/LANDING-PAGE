About EcoSwap

EcoSwap is a platform designed to reduce waste and promote a culture of sharing and sustainability. Whether it’s an old table or extra plastic bottles, you can find someone who can reuse them. We make it easy to exchange, donate, or recycle—right from your home. Together, let’s build a cleaner, greener future by giving waste a second life.
